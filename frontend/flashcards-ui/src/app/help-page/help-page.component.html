Help & FAQs

❓ How do I use flashcards?

Select a deck from the home page. Flip cards, vote on your confidence, and track your progress.

💡 What does the score mean?

Each flashcard tracks how often you've answered confidently (👍). Cards with score > 2 are considered mastered.

📘 What is 'Show Explanation'?

This button reveals a short explanation that reinforces why the answer is correct.

🚀 Can I reset my progress?

This feature is coming soon. For now, refreshing clears temporary state.
